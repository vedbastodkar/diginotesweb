Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy Terms of Service

This Privacy Policy explains how Diginotes ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Diginotes app.

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email address. We store this information securely in your Firebase account to identify you and personalize your experience.

Your notes, checklists, groups, and workspace data are stored in Firebase Firestore under your unique account ID. We do not read or analyze the content of your notes.

How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your identity and maintain your session
  • Store and sync your notes across your devices
  • Send workspace invitations to other users by email
  • Deliver push notifications for workspace task updates

Data Storage

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Authentication), subject to Google's privacy practices. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Authentication session tokens are stored in your device's secure Keychain, not in plain storage.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): data storage and authentication
  • Google Sign-In: optional sign-in method
  • Apple Sign In: optional sign-in method
  • Canvas LMS: optional integration — only accessed with your explicit authorization

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Your note content is never accessed by us or shared with anyone.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us at vedbastodkar@gmail.com. Workspace data shared with other members may be retained until the workspace owner deletes it.

Children's Privacy

Diginotes is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email us at vedbastodkar@gmail.com.
